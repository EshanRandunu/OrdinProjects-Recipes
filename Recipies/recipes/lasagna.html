Watermelon Sangria

Description

Sangria is an alcoholic beverage originating in Spain and Portugal. Under EU regulations only those two Iberian nations can label their product as Sangria

  • red wine
  • orange juice
  • brandy
  • apples
  • blueberries
  • a cinnamon stick

Steps

  1. Start by preparing the fruits. Cut the navel orange into thin slices.
  2. Cut 2 limes into thin slices.
  3. Wash 10-12 strawberries and chop off their top leafy part.
  4. Cut them into small pieces.
  5. Wash the green apple and cut it in half.
  6. Cut the half into small pieces. Use the other half somewhere else.
